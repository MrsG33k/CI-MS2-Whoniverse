How to Play

Welcome to Where in the Whoniverse?! Your mission is to explore the vast and mysterious Whoniverse, matching the Doctor Who scenes to their real life location. Are you ready to embark on this adventure?


You will be presented with a still image from a Doctor Who episode. You will need to:


  • Examine the Location features to find the real life filming location.
  • Use the mini-map to navigate and click to place your TARDIS pin.
  • There are hints for each location to help you on your mission
  • Once ready, Submit your answer to see the distance!
  • Earn points based on your accuracy.
