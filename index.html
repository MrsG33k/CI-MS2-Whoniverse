How to Play

Welcome to "Where in the Whoniverse?"! Your mission is to explore the vast and mysterious Whoniverse, matching the Dr Who scenes to their real life location. you ready to embark on this adventure?

  • You'll be presented with a still image from a Doctor Who episode
  • Examine the "Time-Space Co-ordinates" (Streetview) to find the location you think it was filmed at
  • Use the mini-map to navigate and place your TARDIS pin.
  • Once you think you've found the correct location, submit your answer to see how close you are!
  • Earn points based on how close your guess is to the actual filming location. The closer you are, the more points you earn!

    • Good luck, and may the Whoniverse be with you!
